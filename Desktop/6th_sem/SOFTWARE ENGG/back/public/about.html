ABOUT US




Mittens is a social networking website connecting people, a continuous delivery service at the forefront of an ongoing evolution in communication. We optimize what we do for happiness of our users and ourselves.





Our story began in 2009 when Shrujal and Nishtha, two friends from university, set up the company while working at the same time on their social web startup and for clients. From the beginning we were passionate about both making social websites and the effects great products have on people. We have combined those in creating Mittens, which we launched in 2012, and transitioned to become a major company. Now that we are responsible for a service used in over 100 countries, we feel like we're only getting started.





Doing the right thing Over the years we have grown in all aspects — and continue to every day — but our goals have remained the same. Have fun while working with the best technology at hand. Design and create the finest product we can. Compete with the top in the industry. Learn from the best. Focus on the essential. Cultivate openness and respect in all communication. Be friends with one another. Learn constantly. Share what we know.
